## 🎼 Getting Started with BakaMusic

### Add Your Music to the Player

- Click the “Add Music” or “Open Folder” button inside the app.  
- Choose the folder where your music files are saved.  
- BakaMusic will load all compatible songs automatically.

### Play Songs

- Select any song and click the play button.  
- Use the basic controls for pause, stop, next, and previous.

### Create Playlists

- Click “New Playlist” from the sidebar.  
- Give your playlist a name.  
- Drag and drop songs into the playlist.

### Customize the Player

- Go to the settings menu.  
- Find options for changing themes, layouts, and plugin settings.  
- Enable or disable plugins like equalizers or visual effects.

## ⚙️ Plugin Management

BakaMusic supports plugins to enhance its functions.

### How to Add Plugins

- In the settings, locate the “Plugins” tab.  
- Click “Add Plugin” to browse available plugins.  
- Download and install the ones you want.

### Manage Plugins

- Turn plugins on or off easily from the same tab.  
- Adjust plugin options for better control.

## ❓ Troubleshooting Common Issues

If BakaMusic does not start:

- Make sure your Windows is fully updated.  
- Restart your computer and try again.  
- Check for antivirus software blocking the program.  
- Reinstall BakaMusic following the steps above.

If music files don’t play:

- Confirm the files are in a supported format.  
- Try adding music from another folder.  
- Check the audio output device in the player settings.
